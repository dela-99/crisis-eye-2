Platform Workflow

From Alert to Resolution

CrisisEye connects communities, responders, and coordinators through a single intelligent platform — cutting response time and saving lives.

01

Incident Detected

A citizen, responder, or sensor detects an emergency and submits a structured report with location, severity, and category.

→
02

Alert Broadcast

The incident is classified by priority and broadcast to Ghana Fire Service, Police, Health Service, NADMO, and Ambulance.

→
03

Command & Coordination

The Command Dashboard gives coordinators a real-time picture — active incidents, resources, agency status, and priorities.

→
04

Incident Resolved

Response teams confirm resolution. The incident is logged, metrics are updated, and community status is cleared.
