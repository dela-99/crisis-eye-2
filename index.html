Response Protocol

What to Do in an Emergency

1

Stay Calm

Panic increases risk. Take a breath, assess the situation, and act with purpose. Your safety comes first.

2

Move to Safety

If you are in immediate danger, evacuate the area. Move away from fire, flood zones, or conflict areas before calling.

3

Call Emergency Services

Dial the relevant number above. State your exact location, the type of emergency, and how many people are affected.

4

Report on CrisisEye

After calling, submit a report here so emergency coordinators have full situational awareness of the incident.

5

Assist If Safe

Only provide assistance if it does not put you at further risk. Keep bystanders back and clear the way for responders.

6

Wait for Responders

Stay at a safe distance. Direct emergency teams to the scene and provide accurate information when they arrive.
